Project Details

This is a current Rising Storm 2 Mutator that has been a WIP since April 2021.

What does 29th Extras add?

  • Unlimited roles
  • Custom loadouts
  • AIT's separated into distinct roles
  • New weapons
  • Binos for everyone!
  • A few new cosmetics such as a helmet with customizable rank and unit patches
  • Spawn any weapon with mutate giveweapon
  • Spawn any vehicle with mutate spawnvehicle
  • A new role to drive tanks
  • The ability to damage tanks with a modified RPG
  • Placeable barricades (WIP)
  • Placeable prefabs (WIP)
  • Tanks and helicopters that are not role or team locked
